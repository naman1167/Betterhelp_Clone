Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about BetterHelp's online therapy services

BetterHelp is the world's largest online therapy platform, providing professional mental health support through licensed therapists. We offer convenient, affordable, and private online therapy through text, phone, and video sessions. Our platform connects you with qualified therapists who can help with depression, anxiety, relationships, trauma, grief, and many other mental health concerns.

Founded in 2013, BetterHelp has helped over 3 million people access professional mental health care from the comfort of their homes. Our mission is to make professional therapy accessible, affordable, and convenient for everyone.

BetterHelp is ideal for individuals seeking professional mental health support for various concerns including:

  • Depression and anxiety
  • Relationship issues and couples counseling
  • Stress management and work-life balance
  • Trauma and PTSD
  • Grief and loss
  • Self-esteem and confidence
  • Life transitions and personal growth

However, BetterHelp is not suitable for emergency situations, severe mental health crises, or individuals who require in-person psychiatric care. If you're experiencing a mental health emergency, please contact emergency services or call 988 immediately.

Getting started with BetterHelp is simple and straightforward:

  1. Sign Up: Complete a brief questionnaire about your needs and preferences
  2. Get Matched: Our algorithm matches you with a licensed therapist within 24 hours
  3. Start Therapy: Begin communicating with your therapist through text, phone, or video sessions
  4. Continue Support: Access your therapist anytime through our secure platform

You can switch therapists at any time if you feel the match isn't right for you. Our platform is designed to be flexible and user-friendly, allowing you to focus on your mental health journey.

BetterHelp therapists are all licensed, trained, experienced, and accredited psychologists (PhD/PsyD), licensed marriage and family therapists (LMFT), licensed clinical social workers (LCSW), or licensed professional counselors (LPC). All therapists have a Masters Degree or a Doctorate Degree in their field.

Our therapists have been qualified and certified by their state's professional board after successfully completing the necessary education, exams, training, and practice. They have extensive experience in providing therapy and are equipped to help with a wide range of mental health concerns.

Each therapist is also required to have at least 3 years and 1,000 hours of hands-on experience before joining our platform.

BetterHelp maintains rigorous verification standards for all therapists:

  • License Verification: We verify all licenses with state licensing boards
  • Background Checks: Comprehensive criminal background checks are performed
  • Education Verification: All degrees and certifications are verified
  • Experience Requirements: Minimum 3 years and 1,000 hours of clinical experience
  • Ongoing Monitoring: Regular reviews of therapist performance and client feedback

Additionally, all therapists must complete our platform training and adhere to our professional standards and ethical guidelines. We regularly audit our verification processes to ensure the highest quality of care.

Financial Aid: We offer financial assistance to those who qualify. You can apply for reduced rates during the signup process.

BetterHelp is not directly covered by most insurance plans, but there are several ways to potentially reduce your costs:

  • Out-of-Network Benefits: Some insurance plans offer out-of-network mental health benefits
  • Health Savings Accounts (HSA): You can use HSA funds to pay for BetterHelp services
  • Flexible Spending Accounts (FSA): FSA funds can typically be used for mental health services
  • Financial Aid: BetterHelp offers reduced rates for those who qualify

We recommend contacting your insurance provider to understand your mental health benefits and out-of-network coverage. You can also speak with your BetterHelp therapist about obtaining a receipt for potential reimbursement.

Video sessions on BetterHelp are designed to be simple and secure:

  1. Schedule: Your therapist will schedule video sessions based on your availability
  2. Join: Click the video session link in your BetterHelp account at the scheduled time
  3. Connect: The session runs through our secure, HIPAA-compliant platform
  4. Privacy: Sessions are encrypted and not recorded

Tips for successful video sessions:

  • Find a quiet, private space
  • Test your camera and microphone beforehand
  • Ensure stable internet connection
  • Have a backup plan (phone session) if needed
